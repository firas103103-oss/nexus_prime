## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage Location
- **Database:** Supabase (AWS Asia-Pacific, India)
- **Application:** Railway (Europe West, Amsterdam)
- **CDN:** Cloudflare (global edge network)

### 3.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- **Authentication:** Session-based with HttpOnly cookies
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against brute-force attacks
- **Security Headers:** Helmet.js (CSP, XSS protection)
- **Monitoring:** Sentry error tracking and alerting
- **Backups:** Daily automated backups (30-day retention)

### 3.3 Data Retention
- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Closed accounts:** 90 days after closure (for dispute resolution)
- **Logs:** 30-90 days depending on tier
- **Backups:** 30 days for Starter/Pro, 1 year for Enterprise
